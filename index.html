Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI flashcard maker work?

Enter a topic, a question, or upload a PDF. The AI reads your input, detects the right exam context, and generates a set of flip flashcards with a clear question on the front and a concise answer on the back.

Which exams are supported?

Class 12 CBSE, NEET, JEE, Banking, SSC, and a general "Other" mode for any other subject, exam, or certification.

Can I upload a PDF to make flashcards?

Yes. PDFs are read directly in your browser using PDF.js, and the extracted text is sent to the AI to generate flashcards from the actual content of your document.

Is my data stored anywhere?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Your topic, PDF text, and generated cards are sent only to the Groq API to produce the flashcards and are not stored on any server.
